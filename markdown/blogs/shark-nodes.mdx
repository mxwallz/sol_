# Getting started with Shark Nodes

### What is Shark Nodes?

Shark Nodes provides high-performance RPC node services, enabling developers and businesses to build and scale blockchain applications with ease. Shark Nodes aims to provide fast and reliable RPC node infrastructure, supporting various blockchain networks and use cases.

### Why Shark Nodes?

**Key Advantages:**
- **High-Performance Nodes:** Access fast and reliable RPC nodes for your blockchain applications.
- **Multi-Chain Support:** Use nodes for various blockchain networks.
- **Developer Tools:** Access a suite of tools to enhance your development process.

**Best Features:**
- **High-Performance Nodes:** Deploy nodes for your blockchain projects and applications.
- **Multi-Chain Support:** Select nodes for different blockchains based on your project requirements.
- **Developer Tools:** Utilize tools for debugging, monitoring, and optimizing your applications.

### How to Get Started

**Step 1:** Visit the Shark Nodes website.

**Step 2:** Create an account by signing up with your email.

**Step 3:** Choose a node plan based on your needs.

**Step 4:** Configure your nodes for immediate use.

**Step 5:** Start using Shark Nodes to enhance your blockchain projects.
