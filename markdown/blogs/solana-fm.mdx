# Getting started with Solana FM

### What is Solana FM?

Solana FM is a powerful AI tool designed to discover hidden cryptocurrency gems. It provides users with real-time alerts on promising projects and potential investments.

### Why Solana FM?

**Key Advantages:**
- **Real-Time Alerts:** Get instant notifications on new and promising crypto projects.
- **Advanced Filtering:** Customize filters to receive alerts that match your investment criteria.
- **Community Insights:** Access community-driven data to make informed decisions.

**Best Features:**
- **Real-Time Alerts:** Stay ahead of the market with timely notifications.
- **Advanced Filtering:** Tailor alerts to your specific investment needs.
- **Community Insights:** Leverage data from the community for better decision-making.

### How to Get Started

**Step 1:** Join the Telegram Group by visiting the Solana FM website.

**Step 2:** Set up your alert preferences within the Telegram group.

**Step 3:** Monitor the alerts and act on promising opportunities.

### Pricing and Availability

- **Free Plan:** Basic features available at no cost.
- **Premium Plan:** Access to advanced features for a subscription fee.
